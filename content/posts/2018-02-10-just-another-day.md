You wonder, how resilient  
Are those sunflowers  
At the backyard  
Fighting the winters  
To be able to see  
The sun, afresh.  
I see a sunflower  
Everyday, in you  
Tackling issues  
And waiting in calm  
To be ready to bloom  
In the morning, yet again.
